    Exercise with rope pulley 21 kg 3 sets 10 repetitions Basic exercise 10kg dumbbell in each hand of biceps 3 sets 20 repetitions together with hammer with 12 kg dumbbells 3 sets 20 repetitions

    Shoulder opening 16kg with dumbbell on each side seated 4 sets 10 repetitions Lateral pulley exercise 7kg in each hand 4 sets 10 repetitions
