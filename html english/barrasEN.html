BAR EXERCISE

To vary our exercise routine, we can dedicate at least one day a week to training on the bars, all kinds of calisthenics exercises, triceps dips, biceps dips, muscle-up etc ..., To perform all of them. These exercises require prior preparation, certainty and technique, because they are exercises that must be precise and applied on the muscle that we want to exercise and not on any other, because this way we can harm ourselves and cause possible injuries
